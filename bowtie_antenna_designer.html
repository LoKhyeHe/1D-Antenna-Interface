Model: planar bow-tie, two mirrored circular-sector wings of flare angle α and radius r. Resonant frequency and edge resistance are interpolated from CST-simulated data points at r=50mm, α=40°/80°/120°/160° (Smith & Lim, 2024, Table 1), combined with the paper's validated f₀∝1/r scaling law (checked against their α=160° flare-radius sweep, Figure 4). Substrate loading is a simple bulk εeff=(εr+1)/2 correction. Good for rough sizing/trend estimates only over α∈[40°,160°] — verify final design in CST before submission.
