Substrate & feed
S11 from a real full-wave sweep: every curve is interpolated from an actual NEC2 Method-of-Moments impedance sweep (1.5–4.5 GHz) of wire-grid bow-ties — 55 single-element sizes/flares, 5 patterns × 11 sizes, and 1×1→2×4 arrays with true mutual coupling (one-time ~11 min compute). Substrate εr/thickness apply as a microstrip εeff frequency shift; Z₀ sets the match. Caveats: wire-grid free-space model (no dielectric substrate in the solve), so absolute values are approximate, not CST-grade; gain is still a semi-empirical estimate. The score blends S11 match (40%), −10 dB bandwidth vs the 80 MHz goal (30%) and gain (30%).
